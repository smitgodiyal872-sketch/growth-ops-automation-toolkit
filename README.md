### Day 01: YouTube Revenue Forecaster (Python)

### Day 02: Startup Deal Flow Scorer (Excel System)
* **Problem:** VC/Accelerators drown in noise. Hard to filter top 1% of startups.
* **Solution:** An automated scoring model that ranks startups based on TVL, Team Size, and Growth %.
* **Features:** Weighted Scoring (0-10), Conditional Formatting (Red/Green flags).
